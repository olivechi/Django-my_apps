About TaskFlow

TaskFlow helps you organize your tasks with a clean, colorful interface and simple workflow.

Why TaskFlow?

Track completed tasks, keep pending items visible, and manage your schedule with ease.

  • Modern color palette for better readability
  • Fast task creation and editing
  • Responsive layout for phones and tablets

Built with Django and styled with a crisp color system to make every section stand out.
